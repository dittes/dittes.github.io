Clipboard Definition: What is a clipboard?

In computing, the clipboard definition refers to a temporary storage area in your device's memory. When you copy or cut text, images, or files, they are placed onto your clipboard. This allows you to paste the data into a different location or application.

Traditionally, a clipboard could only hold one item at a time. However, modern operating systems and tools like our clipboard online app allow you to retain multiple items in a running log.

Why use an Online Clipboard Manager?

An online clipboard manager transforms how you handle data. Instead of losing your copied text every time you highlight something new, a manager saves your clipboard history securely in your browser. It serves as a centralized hub to organize, retrieve, and streamline your daily workflow.
