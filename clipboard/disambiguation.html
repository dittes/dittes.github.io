Beyond the Digital: Other Meanings

While our site focuses on software, "clipboard" is a broad term. Here is a guide to physical office supplies, prominent career agencies, and pop culture phenomena sharing the name.

Physical Office Supplies & Dimensions

Before digital screens, the physical writing board was ubiquitous. When shopping for these, understanding clipboard sizes is crucial.

  • Clipboard A4: The global standard for office paper, measuring roughly 8.27 × 11.69 inches.
  • Clipboard 8.5 x 11: The standard US Letter size, commonly used in North American schools and offices.
  • Clipboard with Storage: A favorite among contractors and nurses, featuring a hollow compartment beneath the writing surface to hold extra documents, pens, and forms.

Medical Staffing & Careers

In the healthcare industry, the term is highly associated with a specific tech-enabled staffing platform. Many professionals search for Clipboard Health careers or specific clipboard nursing jobs. This app connects independent healthcare professionals (like CNAs and RNs) with facilities that need per-diem shifts filled.

Gaming, Sports, and Pop Culture

The term appears in surprising places across media:

  • Clipboard 9 Genshin: In the massive open-world game Genshin Impact, players often search for "Static Views" locations. "Clipboard 9" refers to a specific hidden treasure location within the Mysterious Clipboard questline in Sumeru.
  • Clipboard Jesus: A humorous nickname given by sports fans to Charlie Whitehurst, an NFL quarterback known for his long hair and penchant for standing on the sidelines holding a clipboard as a backup player.
