‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is Emoji Clicker?

Emoji Clicker is a free incremental idle clicker game where you tap emojis to earn currency, buy automated buildings, unlock upgrades, and prestige for permanent bonuses. It's playable in any web browser.

How do I play Emoji Clicker?

Click or tap the big emoji to earn emojis. Spend emojis on buildings in the Store tab to automate production. Buy upgrades to multiply your output. Eventually, Reboot to earn Aura for permanent bonuses.

Is Emoji Clicker free?

Yes! Emoji Clicker is completely free to play with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no account required. Your progress saves automatically to your browser.

Does Emoji Clicker work offline?

Yes. Emoji Clicker is a Progressive Web App (PWA). After your first visit, it caches all assets and works fully offline. You can install it to your home screen on mobile or desktop.

What is Aura and how does Prestige work?

Once you've earned enough total emojis (1 billion+), you can "Reboot" to reset your buildings and upgrades in exchange for Aura points. Each Aura gives +1% permanent production and can be spent on the Aura Tree for powerful permanent upgrades.

How do I transfer my save to another device?

Open Settings, click "Export Save" to get a text string, copy it, then on the other device open Settings, click "Import Save", paste the string, and click Load.

Are there any Easter Eggs?

Yes! Emoji Clicker has over 10 hidden easter eggs including secret achievements, hidden modes, and special events. Try exploring unusual interactions to discover them all.
