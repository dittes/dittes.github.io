About Me

I'm Andreas — a product-minded marketeer turned serial entrepreneur. I build at the intersection of software, data, and human experience. Over the past decade I've founded, scaled, and exited multiple companies while trying to push the frontier of technological advances.

My work spans CleanTech, AI, Marketing, Recruiting and FinTech. I believe that the best products are obsessively simple on the outside and ruthlessly engineered on the inside.

When I'm not building, I write about startup strategy, engineering culture, and the art of making good decisions under uncertainty. You can also book me as your keynote speaker ai or other topics like recruiting and innovation.
